The bridge from class

In this week's Instructor Study page, five cells sit on a board and nobody — not even Conway — could predict their fate: the R-pentomino churns for 1,103 generations before it settles. Richard Guy watched it and found the glider; a $50 wager later, Gosper's gun turned one surprise into a machine. Then the triage forge sorts curiosities by a single test: does it hand you a next move?

That's what this page sends into your territory: a useful curiosity gives you a next move. Every lane below has a small thing with unpredictable consequences — find yours.
